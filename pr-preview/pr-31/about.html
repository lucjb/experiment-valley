About Experiment Valley

Experiment Valley is an interactive educational platform designed to teach the fundamentals of A/B testing and statistical analysis through hands-on practice. Our mission is to make complex statistical concepts accessible and engaging for everyone.

Our Mission

We believe that understanding statistical principles is crucial for making informed decisions in today's data-driven world. Experiment Valley bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application by providing:

  • Real-world experiment scenarios
  • Interactive decision-making challenges
  • Immediate feedback and learning opportunities
  • Competition against different decision-making approaches

What You'll Learn

Statistical Analysis

Understanding p-values, confidence intervals, and statistical significance

Experiment Design

Learning about sample sizes, power analysis, and proper experimental setup

Decision Making

Developing skills to make data-driven decisions under uncertainty

Common Pitfalls

Recognizing and avoiding common statistical mistakes and biases

The Learning Experience

Each experiment in Experiment Valley is carefully crafted to simulate real-world scenarios you might encounter in product development, marketing, or business analytics. You'll face challenges that test your ability to:

  • Evaluate experiment trustworthiness
  • Make informed decisions based on statistical evidence
  • Plan appropriate follow-up actions
  • Compare your performance against different decision-making approaches

Competition & Rankings

Track your progress and compete with others through our ranking system. See how your decision-making skills compare to different approaches:

  • HiPPO: Highest Paid Person's Opinion - decisions based on authority rather than data
  • Random: Random decision making without analysis
  • Naive: Simple heuristics that ignore statistical principles
  • Peek-a-boo: Premature stopping and multiple testing issues
